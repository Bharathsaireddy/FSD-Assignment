Procedure for Applying new passport

  1. Make sure you have one proof for your Identity
  2. Adding to that one for your age and one for your address
  3. Take 6 passport size photos with you
  4. Go to Passortseva Kendr website provide all the required details and book an appointment
  5. Go to Passportseva kendr on Scheduled day with all your proofs and photos
  6. Let the officer verify all your proofs
  7. Ola! There you go, in fitten days you will have your passport
